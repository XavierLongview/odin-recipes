Hot Dog

Description

A delicious sandwhich consisting of a meat slurry encased in intestines nestled in a vertically sliced long bun. Traditionally this meal is sauced with a catsup/mustard blend.

Ingredients

  • 1 long bun
  • 1 sausage dog
  • 1 tbs catsup
  • 1 tbs mustard
  1. Boil dog in water for 10 minutes
  2. Toast bun as desired
  3. Assemble dog into bun's vertical slit
  4. Add catsup and mustard to taste
