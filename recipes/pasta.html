Hot Dog

Description

A delicious sandwhich consisting of a meat slurry encased in intestines nestled in a vertically sliced long bun. Traditionally this meal is sauced with a catsup/mustard blend.

Ingredients

  • 277 pieces of pasta
  • 2 quarts water
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1 tbs parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in water for 12 minutes
  2. Drain carefully
  3. Stir in butter and parmesean
