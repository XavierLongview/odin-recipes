Hamburger

Description

This is it, the world famous hamburger. Now you too can finally learn the secrets and make your own. Beef on a bun, that it enough.

Ingredients

  • 1 circle bun
  • 1 beef patty
  • 1 tbs catsup
  • 1 tbs mustard
  1. Cook your beef patty on the grill for 10 mins flipping once
  2. Toast bun as desired
  3. Assemble burger between two horizontal bun slices
  4. Add catsup and mustard to taste
